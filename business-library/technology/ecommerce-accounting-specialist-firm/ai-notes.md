# AI Notes — Nexus Ledger Co.

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose. Educational business-building notes
only — not accounting, tax, or financial advice for any end client._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in finance skills, with analytical and
detail-oriented personality traits. Requires a €10,000-€24,000 budget,
moderate risk tolerance, 30-45 hours/week availability, remote
preference, and no travel requirement. Ideal founder archetypes:
theSpecialist, theConnector.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone precise and technically fluent in e-commerce-
accounting vocabulary (channel reconciliation, landed cost, FIFO,
economic nexus) — never generic small-business bookkeeping language,
and never present content as actual accounting or tax advice.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the three-discipline specialization (multi-channel
reconciliation, landed-cost/COGS, sales-tax-nexus tracking) as the core
differentiator in Offer Architecture, and the higher Moderate-to-high
capital tier in Financial Overview relative to a general bookkeeping
practice — always frame content as business-planning guidance, never as
actual accounting or tax advice.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with the specific mechanics — channel-fee reconciliation, landed
cost, FIFO valuation, nexus thresholds — not generic "we do e-commerce
bookkeeping" stock phrases; this audience wants proof the practice
understands the hardest parts of their books.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as a tiered retainer scaling with connected channels and
order volume, plus a standalone nexus exposure review project fee —
higher price point than a general bookkeeping retainer, justified by
genuinely harder specialist work, with gross-margin/COGS/inventory-
turnover framing rather than burn-rate/runway framing.
