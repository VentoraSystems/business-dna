# AI Notes — Bellwood Realty Group

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose. Educational business-building notes
only._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in sales and negotiation skills, with
persistent and resilient personality traits. Requires a €5,000-€15,000
budget, high risk tolerance, 40-55 hours/week availability, hybrid
preference, and frequent travel for showings. Ideal founder archetypes:
theConnector, theHustler.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone warm, persistent, and locally specific — describe
neighborhoods and transactions the way an agent who's actually walked
the block would, not generic real-estate marketing copy.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the free listing/buyer consultation and the commission-only
income model in the Offer Architecture and Financial Overview sections —
both are what make this business fundamentally different from the
design-fee model of the architecture-studio business elsewhere in this
library.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with specific, walked-the-block neighborhood knowledge and named
past-client outcomes, not generic "your local expert" portal-agent
language.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as lumpy, transaction-dependent commission income with no
base salary, distinct from any retainer or subscription-style business
elsewhere in this library.
