# AI Notes — Millbrook IT Partners

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose. Educational business-building notes
only._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in management and communication skills,
with system-oriented and patient personality traits. Requires a
€12,000-€28,000 budget, low risk tolerance, 40-50 hours/week
availability, hybrid remote preference, and occasional travel. Ideal
founder archetypes: theOperator, theBuilder.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone reassuring, plain-spoken, and non-technical — this
buyer is often an office manager or small-business owner, not an IT
professional.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize 24/7 monitoring coverage and transparent per-seat pricing in
the Executive Summary — predictability, not technical sophistication, is
what this buyer actually cares about.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with a free network-audit offer and the concrete, visible problems
it surfaces, not abstract "IT support" messaging.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as steady, compounding per-seat MRR growth — the most
subscription-like revenue shape of any technology-services business in
this catalog.
