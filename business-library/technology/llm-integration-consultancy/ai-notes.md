# AI Notes — Substrate AI

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose. Educational business-building notes
only._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in programming and AI skills, with
system-oriented and analytical personality traits. Requires a
€22,000-€40,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 40-55 hours/week
availability, remote preference, and occasional travel for client
discovery workshops. Ideal founder archetypes: theBuilder, theSpecialist.

## Generation Hints

Keep tone precise and engineering-credible, concrete about
proprietary-data handling and security posture, and never conflate this
business with a strategy diagnostic or a shared self-serve product — it
sells neither.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize hands-on engineering delivery evidence — shipped systems and
named technical case studies — over strategy-slide credibility in the
Executive Summary, and be explicit that this firm does not sell a
diagnostic and does not sell a shared platform.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with named technical case studies and measurable outcomes
(retrieval accuracy, hours saved, ticket/case volume handled), not
generic "AI agency" messaging — this is a trust sale to technical buyers
(CTOs, Heads of Engineering, General Counsel) who can spot vague AI-hype
copy immediately.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as lumpy, milestone-based fixed-scope project income,
smoothed over time by a growing maintenance-and-model-upkeep-retainer
base — not steady MRR from day one.
