## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in programming and management skills, with
system-oriented and decisive personality traits. Requires a
€15,000-€30,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 40-55 hours/week
availability, hybrid remote preference, and no travel requirement. Ideal
founder archetypes: theBuilder, theOperator.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone confident and technically precise — this audience
(funded founders, mid-market operators) can tell generic AI-agency copy
apart from real engineering credibility.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize delivery discipline — sprint cadence, on-time/on-budget track
record — over raw technical cleverness in the Executive Summary.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with named case studies and measurable outcomes, not generic "we
build software" messaging.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as lumpy, milestone-based project income smoothed over time
by a growing maintenance-retainer base — not steady MRR from day one.
