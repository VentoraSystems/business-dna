# Business Blueprint — Aisle & Away

_Educational business-building guidance only. This document describes
how to build and run a luxury honeymoon planning agency as a business.
Each `##` heading corresponds to one of the 25 sections in
`features/blueprint`'s v2 canonical structure._

## Executive Summary

Aisle & Away is a bespoke luxury honeymoon planning agency for newly
engaged couples, curating private-villa and 5-star-resort stays
through preferred-partner relationships with luxury resorts and
destination management companies (DMCs) — deliberately differentiated
from `adventure-travel-boutique-agency` elsewhere in this category by
occasion (romantic/luxury vs. adventure/expedition), price tier, and
vendor relationships.

## Entrepreneur Fit

Best suited to `theConnector` or `theVisionary` archetype, with at
least three years of relevant experience. Solo-friendly, matching the
adventure agency's precedent.

## Business Overview

A relationship-driven travel-planning agency: couples work with a
single planner from discovery call through in-trip support, with the
agency's value built on preferred-partner access rather than
booking-platform breadth.

## Market Intelligence

Demand comes from newly engaged couples planning a once-in-a-lifetime
trip. The competitive set includes direct hotel/resort booking (cheaper
upfront, but no complimentary upgrades or bespoke curation) and generic
online travel agencies (broad selection, but no luxury preferred-
partner relationships or personal touch).

## Customer Intelligence

Primary customer type is B2C: newly engaged couples planning a luxury
honeymoon, typically discovered through wedding-industry vendor
referrals at the exact moment they're planning post-wedding travel.

## Offer Architecture

The core offer is a fully bespoke itinerary: a discovery call uncovers
the couple's vision and budget, followed by a curated proposal
leveraging preferred-partner relationships for upgrades and amenities,
with hands-on support during the actual trip.

## Revenue Architecture

The planning fee is collected upfront, but the larger supplier
commission is only earned once the full trip is booked and paid for,
typically several months before travel.

## Pricing Strategy

€800-€1,500 upfront planning fee for a fully bespoke itinerary, plus a
10-15% commission or markup built into luxury trips typically priced
€8,000-€25,000+ per couple for a 7-10 day honeymoon.

## Marketing System

Positioning: "your wedding ends, the honeymoon shouldn't feel like an
afterthought." Primary channel is referral partnerships with wedding
planners, photographers, and bridal boutiques; luxury travel content
and honeymoon-registry partnerships are the secondary channel, with a
destination-wedding-planner cross-referral network as an experimental
channel.

## Sales System

A complimentary discovery call uncovers the couple's vision, budget,
and travel style before a fully curated proposal is presented — a
roughly 21-day cycle for a large, emotionally significant purchase.

## Operations System

Core recurring processes: discovery calls and bespoke itinerary
design, preferred-partner booking and upgrade negotiation, ongoing
familiarization-trip relationship maintenance, and real-time in-trip
support.

## Technology Stack

Built on the Axus Travel App for luxury itinerary building and
proposal design, Virtuoso as the luxury travel preferred-partner
network, and WeddingWire as an optional wedding-vendor referral-
sourcing tool.

## Automation Opportunities

Itinerary documentation and booking confirmations are automated
through the travel-booking platform, but discovery, curation,
negotiation, and in-trip support remain fully human-led by design.

## Team Structure

Solo-friendly at launch, with a second planner added once referral
volume exceeds one person's bespoke-attention capacity.

## Financial Overview

Startup budget of roughly €15,000-€35,000 covers luxury-network
membership, familiarization trips, and premium branding — a Medium
budget tier; target monthly income ranges €6,000-€22,000 with an
estimated 12-month path to break-even.

## KPIs

Tracked against discovery-call-to-booking conversion, wedding-
industry-partner referral consistency, gross margin against
familiarization-trip and network-membership costs, and the discovery-
call-to-proposal close rate.

## Risk Analysis

Moderate difficulty with high AI-resistance — the three biggest risks
are the years required to build luxury preferred-partner relationships,
economic downturns compressing the luxury-travel discretionary budget,
and online luxury concierge apps and loyalty programs offering some of
the same perks directly.

## Competitive Advantages

Preferred-partner relationships secure upgrades a couple booking
direct can't access, the wedding-industry vendor referral channel
reaches the exact target audience at the exact moment of need, and
bespoke, hands-on curation provides peace of mind generic booking
platforms can't replicate.

## Launch Strategy

Join a luxury travel preferred-partner network and complete initial
familiarization trips to 2-3 signature destinations, then plan the
first 5-10 honeymoons for couples sourced through initial wedding-
industry relationships.

## 90-Day Action Plan

Weeks 1-6: join a preferred-partner network and complete initial
familiarization trips. Weeks 7-10: plan the first 5-10 honeymoons at a
discounted planning fee. Weeks 11-13: formally launch wedding-industry
vendor partnerships with 3-5 vendors.

## Scaling Strategy

Build deep preferred-partner relationships within a focused set of
luxury destinations first, then expand destination breadth or add a
second planner once referral volume from wedding-industry partners is
consistent.

## Exit Opportunities

Documented preferred-partner network, wedding-industry referral
relationships, and client history make this sellable to a larger
luxury travel agency or hospitality group.

## AI Recommendations

Prioritize AI tools for first-draft itinerary generation — an
implementation accelerator — while keeping discovery, curation, and
preferred-partner negotiation fully human-led given the genuinely
relationship-driven value this business offers.
