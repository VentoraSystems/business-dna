# AI Notes — Vector Tech Talent

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose. Educational business-building notes
only — not employment-law compliance or legal advice for an end client._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in sales and genuinely comfortable with
programming/technical evaluation, with persistent and analytical
personality traits. Requires a €12,000-€30,000 budget, moderate risk
tolerance, 35-50 hours/week availability, fully remote preference, and
no travel requirement. Ideal founder archetypes: theConnector,
theOperator.

## Generation Hints

Tone should be technically credible and precise — use real engineering
vocabulary correctly, avoid recruiter buzzwords, and never overstate the
founder's own coding ability beyond genuine screening competency.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the technical-screening competency and tech-specific sourcing
channels in the Executive Summary and Offer Architecture — the two
clearest differentiators from generalist recruiting agencies, including
the generalist `recruitment-agency` elsewhere in this library.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with concrete technical-screening rigor — the rubric, the
take-home coordination process — and real tech-specific channels like
GitHub and developer communities. Never use generic "IT recruiting"
language.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as placement-driven and lumpy like generalist recruiting,
but with a materially higher average placed salary, which raises the
average fee per placement.
