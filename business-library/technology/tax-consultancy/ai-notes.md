# AI Notes — Ledgerline Tax Advisors

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose. Educational business-building notes
only — not tax advice for an end client._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in finance skills, with detail-oriented
and analytical personality traits. Requires a €6,000-€20,000 budget, low
risk tolerance, 25-60 hours/week availability (heavily seasonal),
hybrid remote preference, and no travel requirement. Ideal founder
archetypes: theSpecialist, theOperator.

## Generation Hints

Tone should be precise and responsive — clients are often anxious
about deadlines and want reassurance paired with competence.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the Enrolled Agent credential and tax-authority representation
rights in the Executive Summary — the clearest differentiator from
unlicensed preparers.

## Marketing Hints

Seasonal urgency around filing deadlines is a legitimate, real driver
here — unlike other regulated businesses, deadline-based messaging is
appropriate.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as seasonally concentrated, not evenly spread across the
year.
