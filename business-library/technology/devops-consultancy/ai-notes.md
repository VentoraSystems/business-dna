# AI Notes — Foundry Lane DevOps

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in programming and AI skills, with
system-oriented and analytical personality traits. Requires a
€10,000-€26,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 35-50 hours/week
availability, remote preference, and no travel requirement. Ideal
founder archetypes: theBuilder, theOperator.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone technically credible and specific about tooling
(Terraform, Kubernetes, CI/CD) — this audience is engineers and CTOs who
can spot generic consulting language immediately.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the incremental, rollback-checkpointed migration approach in
the Executive Summary — the fear of an irreversible production outage is
the single biggest objection this business must overcome.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with specific technical war stories and measurable deployment-time
improvements, not generic "DevOps transformation" consulting-speak.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as fixed-scope infrastructure projects with a smaller,
optional infrastructure-oversight retainer layer — a well-built pipeline
needs less ongoing hand-holding, so retainer conversion runs slower than
in comparable project-based businesses.
