## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in design and communication skills, with
detail-oriented and strategic personality traits. Requires a
€20,000-€60,000 budget, high risk tolerance, 35-50 hours/week
availability, hybrid preference, and occasional travel for site visits.
Ideal founder archetypes: theSpecialist, theVisionary.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone precise and credentialed — describe design decisions
the way a licensed architect explaining code and structural tradeoffs to
a client would, not generic home-design blog language.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the licensure-gated credibility and the percentage-of-
construction-cost pricing option in the Offer Architecture and Pricing
sections — both are what separate this business from an unlicensed
designer or drafting service.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with real completed project photography and named contractor/agent
referral relationships, not generic architectural-style inspiration
content.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as slow-building, long-cycle project fees with a genuinely
high fixed cost base from insurance and licensing, unlike lower-overhead
professional services.
