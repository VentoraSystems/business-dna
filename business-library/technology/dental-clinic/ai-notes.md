## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in management and communication skills,
with detail-oriented and patient personality traits. Requires a
€75,000-€180,000 budget (the highest in this library), moderate risk
tolerance, 45-55 hours/week availability, in-person preference, and no
travel requirement. Ideal founder archetypes: theSpecialist, theOperator.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone calm, reassuring, and strictly business-planning
focused — never generate clinical treatment protocols, diagnostic
criteria, or medical advice of any kind.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the very high capital requirement and licensing/regulatory
compliance in the Financial Overview and Launch Strategy sections, and
the elective-cosmetic margin differentiator in Offer Architecture — keep
all content strictly business-planning, never clinical or diagnostic.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with the modern, unhurried patient experience and named elective
services, not generic "gentle dentistry" stock phrases — never reference
specific clinical outcomes or diagnostic claims.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as a mix of insurance-reimbursed routine care and
higher-margin elective cosmetic services, with equipment financing and
staffing as the dominant cost lines — the highest capital-intensity
business in this library.
