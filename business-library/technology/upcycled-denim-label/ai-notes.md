creative and detailOriented personality traits — a genuine craft
practitioner, not a general e-commerce operator. Requires a
€12,000-€28,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 35-50 hours/week
availability, hybrid work with occasional sourcing travel. Ideal
founder archetypes: theSpecialist, theVisionary.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone authentic and craft-focused, grounded in real
material-sourcing and construction details — never generic
sustainable-fashion marketing language that could apply to any brand.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize genuine craft skill and design vision in Entrepreneur Fit
and Offer Architecture, and the finite deadstock-supply constraint in
Risk Analysis and Financial Overview.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with authentic before/after transformation content and real
material-sourcing stories, never generic "sustainable fashion" stock
phrases.

## Financial Hints

rather than smooth monthly recurring revenue, and treat finite
deadstock supply as a real constraint on production planning.
