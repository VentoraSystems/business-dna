# AI Notes — Meadowlane Beauty Co.

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in marketing and communication skills,
with detailOriented and analytical personality traits — someone who
can both build brand/formulator relationships and personally verify
ingredient-safety claims, not just a general e-commerce operator.
Requires a €16,000-€30,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 30-45
hours/week availability, hybrid work with occasional supplier/
formulator-visit travel. Ideal founder archetypes: theConnector,
theSpecialist.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone warm, reassuring, and grounded in real
ingredient/formulation expertise (dirty-ingredient list, skin-type
matching) — never generic lifestyle-beauty-box language, and never
conflate this with a discovery/novelty subscription model.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize intake-quiz personalization accuracy and clean-ingredient
sourcing/dirty-list verification in Entrepreneur Fit and Offer
Architecture, and the product-fit-mismatch churn risk in Risk Analysis
and Financial Overview.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with concrete skin-type/concern matching accuracy and real
ingredient-transparency detail, never generic "clean beauty box" stock
phrases.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as recurring monthly/bi-monthly subscription income plus
a growing à la carte reorder-shop contribution, and treat per-product
depletion-rate variation — not a uniform monthly-consumable
assumption — as a real constraint on cadence and inventory planning.
