# AI Notes — Crestpoint Talent Partners

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose. Educational business-building notes

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in sales and negotiation skills, with
persistent and decisive personality traits. Requires a €10,000-€30,000
budget, moderate risk tolerance, 35-55 hours/week availability, fully
remote preference, and no travel requirement. Ideal founder archetypes:
theConnector, theHustler.

## Generation Hints

Tone should be energetic and consultative, reflecting genuine sales
strength without sounding pushy.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize niche specialization and the placement guarantee in the
Executive Summary — the two clearest differentiators from high-volume
generalist recruiters.

## Marketing Hints

Referrals from placed candidates convert unusually well and are an
underused channel beyond hiring-manager referrals.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as placement-driven and lumpy, not a smooth recurring
curve.
