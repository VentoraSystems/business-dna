_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in finance and management skills, with
analytical and system-oriented personality traits. Requires a
€4,000-€10,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 30-45 hours/week
availability, remote preference, and no travel requirement. Ideal
founder archetypes: theSpecialist, theBuilder.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone precise and data-driven, fluent in startup-specific
financial vocabulary (burn rate, runway, R&D credits) — never generic
small-business accounting language, and never present content as actual
accounting or tax advice.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the fully remote, automation-heavy delivery model and the Low
capital tier in Financial Overview, and investor-ready reporting
fluency as the core differentiator in Offer Architecture — always frame
content as business-planning guidance, never as actual accounting or
tax advice.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with burn rate/runway fluency and accelerator/VC network
credibility, not generic "we do your books" stock phrases — this
audience wants a partner who speaks startup metrics.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as flat predictable monthly tiers with heavy fintech
automation reducing per-client labor cost — higher margin than a
traditional manual bookkeeping practice, but a more volatile client
base.
