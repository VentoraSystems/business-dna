# AI Notes — Solstice Jewelry Co.

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose. Educational business-building notes
only._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in marketing and design skills, with
creative and detail-oriented personality traits. Requires a
€15,000-€40,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 40-55 hours/week
availability, hybrid preference, and occasional travel for sourcing.
Ideal founder archetypes: theVisionary, theOperator.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone warm and design-confident — describe pieces the way
an experienced jewelry brand founder would, grounded in materials and
craft, not generic ecommerce marketing language.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the try-at-home return window and the ethically-sourced
materials story in the Offer Architecture section — those two elements do
the heaviest lifting against online-purchase hesitation.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with real customer UGC and influencer content showing pieces worn in
daily life, not staged studio-only product shots.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as slow-building direct sales that accelerate through
repeat purchases and gifting-season spikes, with cash flow gated by
inventory production cycles.
