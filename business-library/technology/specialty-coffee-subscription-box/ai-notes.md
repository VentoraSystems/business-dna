# AI Notes — Origin Route Coffee Co.

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in marketing and negotiation skills,
with creative and detailOriented personality traits — a genuine coffee
enthusiast, not just a general e-commerce operator. Requires a
€15,000-€28,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 30-45 hours/week
availability, hybrid work with occasional roaster-sourcing travel.
Ideal founder archetypes: theSpecialist, theConnector.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone warm and genuinely enthusiast-driven, grounded in
real coffee expertise (origin, process, roast profile) — never generic
lifestyle-subscription-box language.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize genuine palate expertise and roaster-relationship sourcing in
Entrepreneur Fit and Offer Architecture, and the inventory-freshness
constraint in Risk Analysis and Financial Overview.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with authentic tasting notes and real roaster stories, never
generic "coffee lover's dream box" stock phrases.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as recurring monthly subscription income with a seasonal
gift-subscription spike, and treat the inventory-freshness window as a
real constraint on how far ahead bean purchasing can be planned.
