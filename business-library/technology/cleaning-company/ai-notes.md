# AI Notes — Brightline Cleaning Co.

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose. Educational business-building notes
only._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in management skills, with detail-oriented
and persistent personality traits. Requires a €1,500-€6,000 budget,
moderate risk tolerance, 35-50 hours/week availability, in-person
preference, and occasional local travel between client homes. Ideal
founder archetypes: theOperator, theBuilder.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone practical, reliability-focused, and concrete about
scheduling and team continuity — avoid generic "sparkling clean home"
marketing filler.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the consistent-team-every-visit promise and the owner-cleans-
first-then-hires operating model in the Offer Architecture and Team
Structure sections — that continuity is the core differentiator from
app-based cleaning marketplaces.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with reliability and the same-team promise, not price — door
hangers and referral discounts in specific neighborhoods outperform
generic "affordable cleaning" messaging.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as fast-starting recurring contracts with labor as the
dominant cost line, distinct from the lumpy commission income of
real-estate-agency elsewhere in this library.
