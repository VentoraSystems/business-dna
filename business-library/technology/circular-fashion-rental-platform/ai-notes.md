# AI Notes — Loopwear Rental Co.

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in management and finance skills, with
systemOriented and detailOriented personality traits — a founder who
can run a tight reverse-logistics cycle at consistent quality, not just
design a rental catalog. Requires a €35,000-€65,000 budget, moderate
risk tolerance, 40-55 hours/week availability, hybrid work with no
travel required (a physical garment-processing and cleaning facility is
core to operations). Ideal founder archetypes: theOperator,
theVisionary.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone operationally grounded and trust-focused, rooted in
real reverse-logistics and fleet-utilization specifics — never generic
sustainable-fashion marketing language, and never language that implies
the customer owns the garment.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the reverse-logistics facility (inspection, industrial
cleaning, minor repair, re-listing) as a real operational step in Offer
Architecture and Operations System, and frame Revenue Architecture
around recurring subscription MRR and fleet-utilization rate — never
treat this as a one-time-sale fashion brand with per-unit COGS.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with cost-per-wear and access-over-ownership messaging and real
reverse-logistics/cleanliness trust signals, never generic "sustainable
fashion" stock phrases and never scarcity/drop-model language that
belongs to a different business model.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as recurring subscription MRR driven by fleet-utilization
rate, not per-unit sale price — frame the rental fleet as a
capitalized, depreciating asset and the reverse-logistics cycle
(cleaning, inspection, repair) as the core recurring cost, distinct
from one-time production or sourcing COGS.
