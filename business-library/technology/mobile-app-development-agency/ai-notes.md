# AI Notes — Copperline Mobile Studio

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose. Educational business-building notes
only._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in programming and management skills, with
system-oriented and decisive personality traits. Requires a
€15,000-€30,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 40-55 hours/week
availability, hybrid remote preference, and no travel requirement. Ideal
founder archetypes: theBuilder, theOperator.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone technically specific about platform differences (iOS
vs. Android vs. cross-platform trade-offs) — this audience can tell a
mobile specialist from a generalist within one sentence.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the studio's App Store and Play Store approval track record in
the Executive Summary — review rejection is the single biggest fear
driving mobile-app buyers to hire specialists.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with a portfolio of live, published apps and their store ratings,
not generic "we build apps" messaging.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as lumpy per-platform project fees smoothed by a growing
OS-compatibility maintenance retainer base, with an added seasonal spike
around major OS releases.
