# AI Notes — Grayridge AI Advisory

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose. Educational business-building notes
only._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in AI and management skills, with
analytical and strategic personality traits. Requires an €8,000-€25,000
budget, moderate risk tolerance, 30-45 hours/week availability, remote
preference, and occasional travel for on-site workshops. Ideal founder
archetypes: theVisionary, theSpecialist.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone sharp, vendor-neutral, and confident — this audience
(COOs, CTOs) is skeptical of hype and allergic to vague AI buzzwords.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize vendor-neutrality and the fixed-fee diagnostic structure in the
Executive Summary — that's what separates this from both an
implementation shop and a slow, expensive big-name consultancy.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with thought-leadership credibility and peer referrals, not paid
acquisition — this is a trust sale, not a demand-generation sale.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as discrete diagnostic-sprint sales, with the advisory
retainer as a smaller, slower-building recurring layer on top.
