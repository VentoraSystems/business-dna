# AI Notes — Loop Chatbot Studio

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in AI/automation and communication
skills, with analytical and system-oriented personality traits.
Requires a €3,000-€10,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 35-45
hours/week availability, remote preference, and no travel requirement.
Ideal founder archetypes: theBuilder, theOperator.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone data-driven and consultative — always ground claims
in ticket-resolution numbers, never vague AI hype language.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize measurable ticket-resolution ROI and the productized
pilot-to-retainer sales motion in Offer Architecture and Sales System,
and the platform-dependency risk in Risk Analysis.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with real resolution-rate case-study numbers, not generic
"AI-powered support" stock phrases — buyers are skeptical of AI support
quality and need evidence.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as a one-time setup fee plus a recurring monthly retainer,
with chatbot-platform subscriptions as the main variable cost — high
margin once the implementation playbook is established.
