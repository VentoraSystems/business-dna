_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in marketing and negotiation skills,
with systemOriented and collaborative personality traits — a founder
who can hold a repeatable, certified supply chain together, not just
design a product line. Requires a €14,000-€30,000 budget, moderate
risk tolerance, 30-45 hours/week availability, fully remote work with
no travel required (manufacturing is fully outsourced). Ideal founder
archetypes: theVisionary, theConnector.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone transparent and evidence-based, grounded in real
farm co-op and certification specifics — never generic
sustainable-fashion marketing language that could apply to any brand,
and never scarcity/drop-model language that belongs to a different
business model.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the direct-trade farm co-op relationship and batch-level
traceability in Offer Architecture and Competitive Advantages, and the
repeatable, scalable core-SKU inventory model (not scarcity drops) in
Revenue Architecture and Operations System.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with verifiable, batch-level supply-chain traceability and real
farm co-op sourcing details, never generic "sustainable basics" stock
phrases.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as a steady, growing e-commerce baseline plus a
compounding subscription-replenishment stream, with predictable
per-unit COGS from a stable certified supplier — not concentrated
bursts around scarce drops, and not variable, opportunistic sourcing
costs.
