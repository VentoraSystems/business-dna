## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in communication and management skills,
with patient and collaborative personality traits. Requires a
€10,000-€30,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 40-50 hours/week
availability, in-person preference, and no travel requirement. Ideal
founder archetypes: theSpecialist, theOperator.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone warm and encouraging, grounded in real
class-pack/membership pricing and the low-impact positioning — never
generic boutique-fitness marketing language.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the Medium capital tier and recurring-membership revenue
model in Financial Overview and Revenue Architecture, and the
low-impact/underserved-niche differentiator in Offer Architecture.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with the low-impact strength/mobility positioning and the
underserved injury-recovery and midlife-fitness niche, not generic
"boutique fitness" stock phrases.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as recurring membership plus class-pack fees, with
instructor wages and studio lease as the dominant cost lines —
meaningful upfront equipment capital relative to a pure service
business.
