# AI Notes — Ridge & Range Adventures

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in communication and sales skills, with
collaborative and risk-tolerant personality traits. Requires a
€10,000-€30,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 35-45 hours/week
availability, hybrid preference, and a frequent travel requirement —
the only business in this library with a genuine, frequent personal
travel requirement. Ideal founder archetypes: theConnector,
theSpecialist.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone enthusiastic but credible, grounded in real
destination and trip-logistics specifics — never generic travel-brochure
language, and never overstate scalability beyond what one founder-led
agency can realistically deliver.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the genuine frequent-travel requirement and firsthand-expertise
positioning in Entrepreneur Fit and Offer Architecture, and the
supplier-reliability risk in Risk Analysis.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with authentic firsthand trip content and real destination
expertise, not generic "bucket list adventure" stock phrases — this
audience can tell the difference between genuine experience and
marketing copy.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as a mix of commission/markup on group trips and flat
planning fees for independent itineraries, with scouting travel and
content production as notable cost lines beyond typical service-business
overhead.
