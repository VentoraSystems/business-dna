# AI Notes — Basalt Coffee Roasters

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in management and communication skills,
with persistent and resilient personality traits. Requires a
€10,000-€30,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 45-55 hours/week
availability, in-person preference, and no travel requirement. Ideal
founder archetypes: theOperator, theHustler.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone warm, energetic, and grounded in real
counter-service specifics — never generic "cozy coffee shop" stock
phrases, and never overstate scalability beyond a single well-run
location.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the fast daily-transaction cash cycle and the Medium capital
tier in the Financial Overview section, and the specialty-quality/
neighborhood-counter differentiator against chain coffee in Offer
Architecture.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with the specialty-quality bean program and the genuine
neighborhood-counter identity, not generic "best coffee in town" stock
phrases — emphasize the daily-ritual occasion, not a destination dining
experience.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as a high volume of small, immediate cash transactions
with bean/milk/labor as the dominant cost lines — the fastest
cash-collection cycle and lowest capital tier among the physical-location
businesses in this library.
