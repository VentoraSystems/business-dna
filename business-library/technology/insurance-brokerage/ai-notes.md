# AI Notes — Harborlight Insurance Brokerage

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in sales and negotiation skills, with
persistent and collaborative personality traits. Requires a
€10,000-€30,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 35-50 hours/week
availability, hybrid remote preference, and occasional travel for
commercial-lines site visits. Ideal founder archetypes: theConnector,
theHustler.

## Generation Hints

Tone should be confident and consultative, reflecting genuine sales
strength — not overly technical or reserved.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize multi-carrier comparison and claims advocacy in the Executive
Summary — the two biggest trust signals over captive agents.

## Marketing Hints

Referrals from real-estate agents and lenders convert unusually well —
worth a dedicated relationship-building effort beyond general referral
marketing.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as renewal-compounding — the client book itself is the
asset, not any single sale.
