## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in automation and technology skills,
with systemOriented and detailOriented personality traits. Requires a
€4,000-€10,000 budget (the smallest in this batch), moderate risk
tolerance, 35-45 hours/week availability, remote work with occasional
travel for onsite process-mapping sessions. Ideal founder archetypes:
theOperator, theSpecialist.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone precise and credibility-driven, grounded in
concrete workflow examples (invoice processing, data reconciliation) —
never conflate this narrow RPA offer with broader AI-automation or
AI-agent language used elsewhere in this library.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the Low budget tier and fast revenue speed in Financial
Overview, and the deterministic-vs-AI-driven trust advantage in
Competitive Advantages — keep the scope deliberately narrow (one
workflow, fixed fee) throughout.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with a specific, visibly painful manual workflow and a concrete
before/after outcome, never generic "automate your business" stock
phrases.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as fixed-fee builds (fast, milestone-paid) plus a smaller
recurring maintenance retainer, not a subscription-first model —
emphasize the low overhead that keeps margin high even at modest
client counts.
