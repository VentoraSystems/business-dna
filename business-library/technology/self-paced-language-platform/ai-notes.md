## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in programming and AI skills, with
creative and systemOriented personality traits. Requires a
€40,000-€70,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 40-55 hours/week
availability, remote work with no travel. Ideal founder archetypes:
theVisionary, theOperator.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone encouraging and habit-focused, grounded in real
streak, spaced-repetition, and AI-tutor specifics — never generic
"learn a language" marketing copy that could apply to any app.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the asynchronous, infinitely-scalable delivery model and
on-demand AI conversation tutor in Offer Architecture and Competitive
Advantages, and lead Marketing System with habit/streak retention
mechanics — the clearest differentiators from the cohort-capped,
live-instruction coding bootcamp elsewhere in this category.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with concrete habit/streak mechanics and real AI-conversation-
practice specifics, never generic "learn a language" stock phrases.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as gradually-building MRR from tiered subscriptions, not
upfront tuition or cohort-batch revenue recognition, and keep growth
explicitly not bounded by any instructor capacity, unlike the
cohort-based coding bootcamp elsewhere in this library.
