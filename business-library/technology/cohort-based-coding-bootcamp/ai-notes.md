## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in programming and communication
skills, with patient and detailOriented personality traits. Requires a
€20,000-€45,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 40-55 hours/week
availability, remote work with occasional travel. Ideal founder
archetypes: theBuilder, theSpecialist.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone encouraging and direct, grounded in real
curriculum and outcomes specifics — never generic bootcamp marketing
language that could apply to any coding school.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the cohort-based live-instruction format and published
job-placement outcomes in Offer Architecture and Competitive
Advantages — the clearest differentiators from self-paced content
platforms this category will eventually include.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with verifiable job-placement outcome data and real
career-changer stories, never generic "learn to code" stock phrases.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as a mix of upfront tuition and delayed ISA collections,
and keep revenue explicitly bounded by cohort capacity rather than
assuming self-paced-platform-style infinite scale.
