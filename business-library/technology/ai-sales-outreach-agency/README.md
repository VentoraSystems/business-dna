## Description

An AI-powered B2B outbound sales agency that builds and runs
personalized cold-email and LinkedIn outreach campaigns for clients
who have a sales team but no dedicated prospecting function,
monetized through a pilot campaign followed by a monthly per-campaign
retainer plus an optional per-meeting performance bonus. Deliberately
distinct from `ai-chatbot-support-studio` (AI-native customer support),
`rpa-workflow-consultancy` (narrow, rules-based back-office
automation), and `ai-automation-agency` (broad AI automation
strategy): this business's entire methodology is demonstrated live on
every prospect through the agency's own outbound campaigns, rather
than described in a pitch deck.

`roadmap.json`, `resources.json`, and `business-insights.json` is a
