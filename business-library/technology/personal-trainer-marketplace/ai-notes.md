# AI Notes — Coachline

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in marketing and negotiation skills,
with systemOriented and collaborative personality traits. Requires a
€35,000-€80,000 budget, high risk tolerance, 40-55 hours/week
availability, hybrid work with occasional travel. Ideal founder
archetypes: theBuilder, theConnector.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone trustworthy and responsive, grounded in real
certification-verification specifics — never generic "find a trainer
near you" stock phrases that could apply to any listing site.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the certification-plus-liability-insurance verification
standard and the gym-partnership supply channel in Offer Architecture
and Marketing System — the clearest differentiators from the tutoring
and home-repair marketplaces elsewhere in this category.

## Marketing Hints

Always present demand-side client acquisition and supply-side
trainer/gym-partner recruitment as two distinct marketing motions,
never collapse them into a single generic plan.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as commission on session-booking value plus a
gym-partner revenue share, not a flat fee or subscription, and keep
the liability-insurance reserve visible as a real, distinct cost line
given physical-injury stakes.
