# AI Notes — Clearpath Leadership Coaching

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose. Educational business-building notes
only._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in communication and management skills,
with collaborative and patient personality traits. Requires a
€2,000-€6,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 25-40 hours/week
availability, remote preference, and no travel requirement. Ideal founder
archetypes: theSpecialist, theConnector.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone grounded and practical — describe real management
scenarios the way an experienced people-manager-turned-coach would, not
generic motivational coaching language.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the free discovery call and the company-sponsor dynamic in the
Offer Architecture and Sales System sections — a second stakeholder
(HR/L&D) is often part of the buying decision.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with specific, real first-time-manager scenarios and how to handle
them, not generic "unlock your leadership potential" language.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as package-based sales with quarterly renewal cycles for
company-sponsored engagements, recognizing the founder's own calendar as
the hard capacity ceiling.
