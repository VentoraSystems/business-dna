## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in content and communication skills,
with detailOriented and patient personality traits. Requires a
€4,000-€10,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 30-45 hours/week
availability, fully remote work with no travel requirement. Ideal
founder archetypes: theSpecialist, theOperator.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone warm, reliable, and craft-focused, grounded in
real audio-production specifics — never generic content-agency
language that could apply to any media format.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the Low budget tier and fast revenue speed in Financial
Overview, and the referral-from-guests advantage in Marketing System
and Competitive Advantages — keep the show-abandonment churn risk
concrete in Risk Analysis.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with actual production-quality samples, never generic "we
produce podcasts" stock phrases.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as a show-launch package converting into a recurring
per-episode retainer, and treat client churn from abandoned shows as a
real, ongoing revenue-stability risk to plan around.
