# AI Notes — Fernway Marketing Group

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose. Educational business-building notes
only._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in marketing and content skills, with
creative and collaborative personality traits. Requires a
€4,000-€9,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 35-50 hours/week
availability, remote preference, and no travel requirement. Ideal
founder archetypes: theConnector, theHustler.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone data-literate and confident — always ground claims in
named case studies and concrete metrics rather than generic "results
driven" agency language.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the free funnel audit and the three-month minimum retainer
commitment in the Offer Architecture and Pricing sections — that pairing
is the strongest protection this business has against premature client
churn.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with specific named case studies and their exact before/after
metrics, not generic "full-funnel marketing" language — a board-level
growth number is the only thing this ICP actually cares about.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as a genuine monthly retainer from the first signed client,
not a project fee with an optional retainer bolted on afterward — this is
the deliberate structural difference from the technology-services
businesses in this library.
