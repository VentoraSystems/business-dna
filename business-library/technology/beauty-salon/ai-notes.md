# AI Notes — Lumière Hair & Beauty Studio

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose. Educational business-building notes
only._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in design/craft skills, with creative and
detail-oriented personality traits. Requires a €10,000-€30,000 budget,
moderate risk tolerance, 40-55 hours/week availability, in-person
preference, and no travel requirement. Ideal founder archetypes:
theSpecialist, theOperator.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone warm, craft-focused, and specific about real services
and stylists — avoid generic "pamper yourself" spa marketing language.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the consistent-stylist-relationship promise and the chair-based
team structure in the Offer Architecture and Team Structure sections —
this is a craft and relationship business, not a walk-in commodity
service.

## Marketing Hints

Lead with real before/after client transformations and named stylists,
not generic "luxury salon experience" stock imagery.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as fast, same-day service collection with lease and
stylist compensation as the dominant cost lines, distinct from the
project-based revenue of professional-service businesses elsewhere in
this library.
