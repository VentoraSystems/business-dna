# AI Notes — Tradeline Home Services Marketplace

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s
`aiMetadata` field) in plain prose._

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in management and negotiation skills,
with systemOriented and resilient personality traits — this is not a
solo-friendly business, and requires operations discipline across two
distinct customer populations plus real contractor-liability
management. Requires a €40,000-€90,000 budget (the largest in this
batch), high risk tolerance given the genuine cold-start liquidity
challenge and liability exposure, 45-60 hours/week availability,
hybrid work. Ideal founder archetypes: theOperator, theConnector.

## Generation Hints

Keep generated tone trust-building and safety-conscious, grounded in
real marketplace mechanics — this is a two-sided marketplace business,
so every generated section should distinguish demand-side (homeowner)
dynamics from supply-side (contractor) dynamics rather than treating it
as a one-sided service.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize the cold-start liquidity problem and the "not solo-friendly"
team requirement in Entrepreneur Fit and Launch Strategy, and the
contractor liability/insurance-verification obligation in Risk Analysis
and Operations System — this is the second genuine two-sided
marketplace in the library and should read differently from the
one-to-one service businesses elsewhere.

## Marketing Hints

Always present channel strategy as two distinct motions — demand-side
homeowner acquisition and supply-side contractor recruitment — never
collapse them into a single generic "marketing plan."

## Financial Hints

Model revenue as commission on gross merchandise value (completed job
value) plus an optional job-guarantee fee, not a flat fee or
subscription; net margin should stay noticeably lower than the
library's one-sided service businesses to reflect genuine two-sided
platform overhead, and break-even should reflect the real time
two-sided liquidity takes to build market by market.
