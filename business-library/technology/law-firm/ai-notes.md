# AI Notes — Fairview Legal Partners

_Mirrors `features/business-dna`'s `AiMetadata` (`business-dna.json`'s

## Matching Hints

Best matched to someone strong in negotiation and communication skills,
with detail-oriented and analytical personality traits. Requires a
€15,000-€45,000 budget, moderate risk tolerance, 35-55 hours/week
availability, hybrid remote preference, and occasional travel. Ideal
founder archetypes: theSpecialist, theOperator.

## Generation Hints

Tone should be precise, measured, and confidence-inspiring — never
casual.

## Blueprint Hints

Emphasize malpractice coverage and flat-fee predictability in the
Executive Summary — the two strongest trust signals for a small-business
audience.

## Marketing Hints

Referrals from accountants and consultants convert far better than any
advertising for this audience.

## Financial Hints

Model revenue with a hard hourly-capacity ceiling until a second
attorney is added.
